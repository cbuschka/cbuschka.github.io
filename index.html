  • Education

  • Logistics
  • Medical Research
  • Publishing
  • Shipbuilding
  • Stock Exchange
  • Tourism
  • Translation.

Currently I am an employee at Otto (GmbH & Co KG).

While most fluent in

  • Java,
  • Javascript,
  • and Python
I feel at home in the frontend and backend, and do operations as well.

I love k8s, docker, ansible, Linux, make, the command line, maven, the spring framework, PostgreSQL, S3, async messaging, React, DDD, CQRS/EDA, feature toggles, ...

Currently I prefer working in the cloud (AWS, Scaleway, GCP), but I am also experienced with taming on premise infrastructures.

I have a strong foundation in isolating, modelling and implementing business logic. And I really know how to organize software to keep it maintainable for years.

Though I love building software from scratch, my heart beats for modernizing legacy systems.
